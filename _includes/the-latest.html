The Latest

The U.S. Department of the Treasury released the next minor update of the DATA Act Information Model Schema (DAIMS). DAIMS version 1.2 will be implemented with the submission of fiscal year 2018 quarter 3 data. Find out more at the DAIMS v1.2.

The U.S. Department of the Treasury has recently released the alpha version of the DATA Lab at USAspending.gov, an initiative to create visualizations and analyses using federal spending data.

The U.S. Department of the Treasury has launched Beta.USAspending.gov! Explore, search, and better understand how the federal government spends taxpayer dollars. Take a look and tell us what you think.
